# Genie

Generative Natural Language Inference Engine Artificial Intelligence (GenAI) is all the rage at the moment. Its a new
generation of general capability that everyone is exploring for it's application in their domain. Genie is sn example of
applying GenAI to the domain of software development.

Genie is a CLI assistant that uses GenAI to provide assistance to developers in their day to day tasks. It is a CLI tool
that can be integrated into other workflows in scripts or command line.
